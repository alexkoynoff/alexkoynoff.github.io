Summary: Latitute vs. Wind Speed

Based on the random sample of 500+ cities, the wind speed tends to be slightly higher for cities that are above the equator, compared to below the equator during the time of this sample, April 2019. This can be seen in the higher number of cities that are above 20 mph, located above the equator.
